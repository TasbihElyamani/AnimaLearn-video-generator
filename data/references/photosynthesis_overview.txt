Photosynthesis converts sunlight into chemical energy: 6CO2 + 6H2O + light → C6H12O6 + 6O2. Occurs in chloroplasts. Two stages: light-dependent reactions (thylakoids, produce ATP/NADPH, release O2) and Calvin cycle (stroma, fix CO2 into glucose). Chlorophyll absorbs red and blue light. Produces ~50-80% of Earth's oxygen.
