SAMPLE SCRIPT — How to Train a Neural Network (1 min)
Step 1: Prepare labeled data (images with categories, text with labels).
Step 2: Define architecture (layers, neurons, activation functions).
Step 3: Forward pass — feed data through the network.
Step 4: Compute loss — how wrong are the predictions?
Step 5: Backpropagate — compute gradients.
Step 6: Update weights using an optimizer like Adam.
Step 7: Repeat for many epochs. Monitor validation loss.
Step 8: Deploy the trained model.
