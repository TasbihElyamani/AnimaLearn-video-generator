Attention allows models to focus on relevant parts of input. Bahdanau attention (2014): additive attention for seq2seq. Luong attention: dot-product based. Self-attention: each position attends to all others in the same sequence. Multi-head attention: parallel attention with different projections. Cross-attention: attends to a different sequence (encoder output). Attention weights are interpretable, showing what the model 'looks at'.
