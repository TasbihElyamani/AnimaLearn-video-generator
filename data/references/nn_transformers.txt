The Transformer Architecture

Introduced in 'Attention Is All You Need' (Vaswani et al., 2017). Core mechanism: scaled dot-product attention: Attention(Q,K,V) = softmax(QK^T/√d_k)V. Multi-head attention runs attention in parallel with different learned projections. Architecture: encoder-decoder with positional encoding, layer normalization, feed-forward networks, and residual connections. Key advantage: processes all tokens in parallel (unlike sequential RNNs). BERT (2018): encoder-only, bidirectional, pre-trained on masked language modeling. GPT series: decoder-only, autoregressive, pre-trained on next-token prediction. T5: encoder-decoder, text-to-text framework. Vision Transformer (ViT): applies Transformers to image patches. Scaling laws show performance improves predictably with more parameters, data, and compute.
